Baked Salmon

Description:

Succulent, flaky and all around nutritious, baked salmon is a quick and easy way to enjoy one of nature's greatest bounties. All you need to make this delicious dish is baking pan an oven and a tiny bit of patience.

The baked salmon we will be making today is very simple and straightforward. Start by pre-heating your oven to 350 degrees F and greasing your baking pan unless it is non-stick. Season the salmon with salt and pepper. All thats left is to place the salmon in the oven for 15 minutes and let the salmon work its magic. Once removed from the oven garnish with parsley and lemon zest. Voila you have a delicious baked salmon!

Ingredients:

  • Salmon (16 oz filet)
  • Coarse ground salt
  • Fresh Cracked Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 lemon

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Grease pan with olive oil
  3. Season salmon to taste
  4. Place salmon in oven once its at temp
  5. Let salmon cook for 15 min or until desired doneness
  6. Remove salmon from oven zest lemon and enjoy!

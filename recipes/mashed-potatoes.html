MASHED POTATOES

DESCRIPTION

You hear the turkeys gobblin, the mixer mixin', the butter meltin'. That's right boys its mashed taters. The perfect side to accompany any meal, well most meals. What could be better than fluffy, buttery, garlicy mashed potatoes on a cold winter day!?

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Bring 2 quarts of water to a boil, salt generously.
  2. Cut 3 russet potatoes into quarters.
  3. Soak potatoes in cold water for 3 minutes.
  4. Drain off potatoes and place into pot of boiling water.
  5. Once fork tender, remove potatoes from water and drain.
  6. Place 1 stick of butter, and half cup of milk into pot over low heat.
  7. Once the butter is melted, place potatoes into the pot and begin to mash.
  8. Mix in 1/4 cup of parmasean cheese, garlic powder, pepper, and salt.
  9. EAT THOSE THANGS!

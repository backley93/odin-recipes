BAKED SALMON

DESCRIPTION

What could be better than flaky, succulent salmon? Nothing. Absolutely nothing. We love salmon its an S-tier food. What more is there to say?

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 F
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  3. Place foil on pan and lightly spread olive oil on it.
  4. Place salmon filet, skin-side down on foil, and put in the oven.
  5. After 15 minutes or when at desired doneness, remove from oven.
  6. Garnish with lemon and parsley.
  7. Enjoy!

Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Description:

What could accompany a dish better on a cold winter day than a nice hearty helping of roasted brussel sprouts. These tasty veggies are seasoned with salt and pepper and can accompany a wide variety of dishes.

In order to cook these tasty sprouts you won't need much more than an oven, some oil and some seasoning. Follow the recipe below to enjoy one of nature's best offerings!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of brussel sprouts
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder(for added flavor)

Directions:

  1. First preheat your oven to 425 F with your baking sheet inside
  2. Halve the brussel sprouts and rinse them thoroughly
  3. Dry the brussel sprouts
  4. In a mixing bowl toss sprouts with oil and seasonings
  5. When the oven reaches temp pull the pan and place the sprouts onto the pan
  6. Set the oven timer for 15-20 minutes
  7. Halfway through the cook turn over the sprouts
  8. When timer ends remove from the oven and allow sprouts to cool
  9. Enjoy!

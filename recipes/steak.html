PAN-SEARED STEAK

DESCRIPTION

Steak, the man's meal. If you like it well-done, then please go back to the salmon page. This is the food of the gods. There is simply nothing better than a steak cooked to medium-rare perfection... well maybe something is better, but that ain't food.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Once pan is smoking, pour generous amount of neutral oil on the pan.
  4. Place the steak away from your body down on the pan.
  5. Sear for ~3 minutes each side until a nice crust has formed.
  6. Place butter, thyme and garlic in the skillet, and begin basting the steak for 1 minute on each side.
  7. Rest steak on cutting board for 10 minutes.
  8. Carve and enjoy!

Butter-Basted Steak

Description:

The man's meal! What could be better than a perfect medium rare butter basted steak? Make sure to cook on a cast iron pan to get the perfect sear!

To bring out the flavor of the steak, we'll baste it in butter, herbs and garlic. Don't forget to let your steak rest to ensure that every bite is perfect. Here's what you'll need to pull off one of the most desired achievments in the culinary world, the perfect steak!

Ingredients:

  • 16oz ribeye steak
  • Salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3 sprigs of thyme
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • 4 tbsp of neutral high burn point oil

Directions:

  1. Set your stove to medium high heat
  2. Place your cast iron skillet on the stove to preheat
  3. Oil the skillet
  4. Season the steak with salt and pepper
  5. Once the oil starts to smoke place the steak on the skillet
  6. Get a hard crust on both sides of the steak
  7. Place butter, thyme and garlic in the skillet
  8. Begin basting the steak until the whole surface is brown and external fat begins to render
  9. Take the steak out of the pan and let rest for ten minutes
  10. Enjoy!
